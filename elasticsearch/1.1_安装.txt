	
	1.下载elasticsearch，解压运行
	  https://www.elastic.co/guide/en/elasticsearch/reference/current/_installation.html
	  进入 http://localhost:9200/

	2.安装head插件
	  https://github.com/mobz/elasticsearch-head
	  进入 http://localhost:9200/_plugin/head/
	  
	3.安装kibana
	  https://www.elastic.co/downloads/kibana
	  进入 http://localhost:5601
	  
	4.安装marvel插件
	  https://www.elastic.co/guide/en/marvel/current/installing-marvel.html
	  elasticsearch: https://download.elastic.co/elasticsearch/release/org/elasticsearch/plugin/license/2.3.1/license-2.3.1.zip
	  elasticsearch: https://download.elastic.co/elasticsearch/release/org/elasticsearch/plugin/marvel-agent/2.3.1/marvel-agent-2.3.1.zip
	  kibana: https://download.elasticsearch.org/elasticsearch/marvel/marvel-2.3.1.tar.gz
	  进入 http://localhost:5601/app/marvel
	  
	5.安装Sense
	  https://github.com/bleskes/sense
	  进入 http://localhost:5601/app/sense
	
	6.安装ik分词器
	  https://github.com/medcl/elasticsearch-analysis-ik
